# EquiSports: (A Sports Equipment Store)
Welcome to the **EquiSports (A Sports Equipment Store)** Project! Our goal is to create a seamless and responsive e-commerce website where customers can easily browse, purchase, and review a wide range of sports accessories. From gear to apparel, we aim to offer products for various sports disciplines, catering to athletes of all levels.
